Virtual Reality is a simulated experience that plays out hyperreality through devices.


This means that what you're seeing can be similar to or completely different from the real world.

Here's a glimpse into the future:


Opinions differ on what exactly constitutes a true VR experience, but generally are catagorized to include:

  • Three-dimensional images that appear to be life-sized by the user's perspective

  • Have the ability to track a user's motions

  • Correspondingly adjusts images to reflect the change in perspective.

Unexpectedly, VR is currently being used in disciplines such as:

  • ✧ Architecture Modeling ✧

  • ♚ Sports ♚

  • ❈ Medical Practice ❈


With VR, our perception turns into a virtual scenario that uses our 5 senses. Using several complex programs, the technology can become interdisciplinary whether its uses in entertainment or serious purposes. As VR becomes more affordable and vast, society can expect it to become a fundamental way in which we communicate, practice, and work.
